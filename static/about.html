About

I'm a development team lead working at Circular Wave in London, England, where we're striving to eradicate inefficiencies in healthcare through innovation and technology.

Outside of software development I've had several year's experience working as a technical author, a technical illustrator and an interface designer for the engineering industry. A long time ago I also worked in film and TV post production.

I'm hungry to learn, and I have a big interest in teaching and sharing what I learn, which is largely the reason for this site's existence.

I live in Kent with my wife and two sons.
